# Hi, I'm Khagesh Chaturvedi 👋

Financial crime and compliance professional, now building at the intersection of traditional banking and Web3.

I spent the better part of two decades inside India's largest banks — reviewing suspicious transactions, managing AML/KYC operations across high-volume branches, and training teams to spot fraud before it happens. These days I apply that same lens to DeFi, crypto compliance, and on-chain risk.

--
## 🔍 What I work on

- **AML & KYC** — policy design, EDD frameworks, SAR/STR reporting
- **Crypto Compliance** — VASP regulation, Travel Rule, DeFi risk assessment
- **OSINT & Financial Crime Investigation** — on-chain tracing, typology research
- **Trade-Based Money Laundering (TBML)** — detection, red flag indicators, typologies
- **Web3 Education** — making compliance accessible for blockchain-native audiences
